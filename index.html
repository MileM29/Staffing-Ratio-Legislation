What Does Staffing Policy Mean for Patients?

- Every additional patient a nurse takes on carries an additional 12% odds of in-patient mortality.


- Higher staffing levels are associated with improved patient outcomes, including reduced mortality and fewer failure-to-rescue events.


- Strong staffing levels can reduce patient length of stay, lowering system-wide costs while improving overall quality of care.
